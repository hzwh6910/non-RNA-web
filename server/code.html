Datasets

We extract four different RNA subcellular localization datasets, including mRNAs, lncRNAs, miRNA and snoRNAs. There are 13475 mRNAs sequences with 9 subcellular locations, 965 lncRNAs sequences with 5 subcellular locations, 488 miRNAs sequences with 6 subcellular locations and 207 snoRNAs sequences with 5 subcellular locations, respectively. We also extract four different Homo sapiens RNA subcellular localization datasets,including H_mRNAs,H_lncRNAs,H_miRNA and H_snoRNAs. There are 8342 H_mRNAs sequences with 5 subcellular locations, 588 H_lncRNAs sequences with 5 subcellular locations, 305 H_miRNAs sequences with 6 subcellular locations and 191 H_snoRNAs sequences with 5 subcellular locations, respectively. And the datasets can be download here.
