Different types of RNA have different functions, so they carry different messages.In order to better distinguish the subcellular Location of specific types of RNA, we provide four different types of RNA servers, including mRNA Location, lncRNA Location, miRNA Location and snoRNA Location. You can click on different servers to identify the Location of subcells.
